Financing Description

To create a proforma financing worksheet, simply append three variables to the end of the url separated by commas. Order is significant. The application will determine the price per share, new share quantities, option pool increase, and calculate the diluted stakes of all shareholders. The appended variables are:

Total Raise
The amount of money, in dollars, you are raising.
Prevaluation
the prevaluation, in dollars, of the round.
Desired Option Pool
The total amount, in basis points, of the fully diluted option pool available after the financing is complete. All options will be calculated "in the pre". If you do not wish to expand the option pool then simply append a 0.
